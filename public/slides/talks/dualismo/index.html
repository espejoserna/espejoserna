Dualismo cartesiano

Dualism in philosophy of mind is the theory that the mind is distinct from the body; it is the theory that a human being comprises not only a physical body, generally encompasing torso, limbs, head, and neck, but also an immaterial mind, which is seen as the seat of thought and conscious experience
